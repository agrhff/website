Planet lover

Project overview

For this project, I was provided with a brief to design and develop a brand new e-commerce website for Planet Lover. Planet Lover is an eco-brand that offers stylish and sustanaible solutions to replace plastic packaging. While the primary goal of the new e-commerce website was to allow customers to purchase products online, the site also needed to distinguish Planet lover from other eco-friendly brands by emphasizing their highly curated inventory of handmade items.

Role

UI designer & front-end developer (Shopify)

Project time

3 weeks

Website

planetlover.co

Goals

The primary business goals for this website included:

  • Having clear ways of locating products
  • Clear and minimalistic navigation
  • Having an efficient means of purchasing one or more products
  • Steering customers towards another similar products in their store

Challenge

Client has a tight deadline: 3 weeks to design and develop from scratch a ready-to-go online store corresponding to all business goals and user needs.

Solution

To be able to meet deadline I prepared a project brief plan, comprising all important functionality, and a detailed plan to add other features step by step after launch.

Competitive research

I began research by identifying the main three competitors in the Canadian eco-friendly market and comparing their online stores. I was keen to identify common features across the sites and spot opportunities for Planet Lover to be unique. The competitors I decided to focus on were Olsen + Olsen, Zero waste mvmt, and DemainDemain. With each providing slightly different products but similar customer demographics. Hero section with a call to action, bestsellers and featured collection on the homepage were common features on all three websites. In addition, they all included a cart, profile, store information and newsletter form in the footer.
